Food and Recipies for Around Provo

9 Places for Halloween Meal Deals

  1. Baskin Robbins

    2. Head over to Baskin Robins and get a cone or cup of ice cream for only $1.50


  2. Burger King

    3. Feeling Silly? Head over to your local burger king. The first 500 people dressed as clowns will get a free Whopper.


  3. Chick Fil-A

    4. Depending on location, Chick Fil-a is giving away original chicken sandwiches with any purchase of large fries and drinks


  4. Chipotle

    5. Stroll on into your local Chipotle and get a Burrito for only $3!

  5. Krispy Kreme

    6. Krispy Kreme is giving a free donut to anyone that walks into the store with a costume

  6. Sonic

    7. Drive into your local Sonic for 50 cent corndogs

  7. Tucanos

    8. Tucanos offers dinner at lunch prices on Halloween
